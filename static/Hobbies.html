Computer Science

I am a computer engineer and I like computers, specially when they work as I want. However, my computer does not use to work as I'd like. Here are some points I specially hate in my work with computers:

  • Printing: Where is that beautiful printer with paper, toner and ready to print?
  • Installing: I don't like versionitis
  • Virus: I really don't know if my operating system is a whole virus
  • Waiting: My computer is very very quick, but, why do I have to be waiting for it so many times?
  • Rebooting: It is closely related with number 2
  • Networking: Yes! My computer prefers to work alone and doesn't like to communicate with others.
  • Computer Stupidity: my computer doesn't look very intelligent. It always repeat the same stupid operations in the same order.

Spanglish

I find spanglish fun. I have tried to write these pages in english, although it could be considered spanglish. Some reasons why I write in english (spanglish):

  • It is easier to accept a new idiom than to fight against it.
  • In my view, the arguments that defend the old languages (english or spanish) are dictatorial and conservative.
  • I am very very lazy to update 2 pages with the same contents (English and Spanish)
  • I also want to practicar my english. This is a good way to try to use the idiom and if someone says that I misused some word, I have the excuse that I was using spanglish!
  • I have the same reasons that made Kactus Jack to sing in spanglish, sometimes, it sounds/looks better and the audience may be bigger.
  • You probably think I am very snob for using english and not spanish. But then why not Asturian? And then update 3 different pages...oh! It looks a lot of work, if you want to update my pages in those idioms, I will be very grateful for your help :)
